From 2006 admissions onwards, MSIT has introduced the "Walk-in Entrance" (now from home itself). This is planned for the convenience of the students where the students have an opportunity to take the MSIT entrance exam, whenever he/she is confident of taking the exam.

Walk-in Entrance Test (now online and from home itself):

Candidates have the option to choose the date of the exam. Walk-in entrance test can be taken on your convenient date and time between May 15tth to June 26th. But only once. A slot from 10 AM to 11AM OR 2.00PM to 3 PM on any day between May 15th to June 26th can be opted. A non- refundable amount of Rs.1000 has to be paid through online by Credit / Debit card or net banking from our website(www.msitprogram.net).
